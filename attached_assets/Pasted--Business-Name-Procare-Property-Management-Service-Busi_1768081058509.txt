*Services Offered*

1. Property Management

We handle the day-to-day management of residential and commercial properties, including tenant coordination, rent monitoring, inspections, and general oversight to ensure the property is well managed and profitable.



2. Facility Management

We ensure the smooth operation and maintenance of building facilities such as electrical systems, plumbing, water supply, generators, and common areas, keeping properties safe, functional, and well maintained.



3. Construction Project Supervision & Management

We supervise and manage construction and renovation projects from start to finish, coordinating contractors, monitoring quality of work, ensuring proper use of materials, and making sure projects are delivered on time and within budget.



4. Property Maintenance & Repairs

We provide routine and emergency maintenance services, including electrical works, plumbing, painting, carpentry, and general repairs to preserve property value and extend building lifespan.



5. Construction Contractor

Procare undertakes building and renovation works as a contractor, delivering quality construction services in line with approved drawings, specifications, and industry standards.



6. Short-Let & Apartment Management

We manage short-let apartments by handling guest check-ins and check-outs, cleaning, maintenance coordination, and general operations to ensure high occupancy and guest satisfaction.



7. Cleaning Services

We offer professional cleaning services for residential, commercial, and short-let properties, including post-construction cleaning, routine cleaning, and deep cleaning services.

8. Sales & Marketing of Property
 We handle the sales, leasing, and marketing of properties by promoting listings, managing inspections, engaging prospective buyers or tenants, negotiating deals, and ensuring smooth transaction processes for property owners.
